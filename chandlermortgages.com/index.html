Frequently Asked Questions About Mortgages

What types of home loans are available?

We offer several mortgage options including: - Conventional loans (fixed and adjustable rate) - FHA loans (low down payment) - VA loans (for veterans and military) - Jumbo loans (for high-value properties) - Refinancing options

Each loan type has different requirements and benefits we can explain.

What are current mortgage rates?

Mortgage rates change daily based on market conditions. As of today, our rates are: - 30-year fixed: 6.25% - 15-year fixed: 5.75% - 5/1 ARM: 5.50%

Contact us for personalized rate quotes based on your credit score and loan scenario.

How much down payment do I need?

Down payment requirements vary by loan type:

  • Conventional loans: Typically 5-20%
  • FHA loans: As low as 3.5%
  • VA loans: 0% down for qualified veterans
  • Jumbo loans: Usually 10-20%

We offer down payment assistance programs for qualified buyers.

How long does the mortgage process take?

The typical mortgage timeline is:

  1. Pre-approval: 1-3 days
  2. Home search: Varies
  3. Underwriting: 2-3 weeks
  4. Closing: 30-45 days total

We offer accelerated processing for qualified buyers who need to close quickly.

What credit score do I need to qualify?

Minimum credit scores vary by loan type:

  • Conventional: 620+
  • FHA: 580+ (500-579 with 10% down)
  • VA: No minimum (but lenders typically want 620+)
  • Jumbo: 700+

We work with borrowers across the credit spectrum and can suggest ways to improve your score.
