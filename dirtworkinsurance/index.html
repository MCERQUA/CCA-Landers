Frequently Asked Questions About Dirtwork Insurance

What types of businesses does dirtwork insurance cover?

Dirtwork insurance covers excavation contractors, grading companies, site preparation contractors, foundation specialists, trenching businesses, and any other contractors involved in earthmoving operations.

How much does specialized excavation insurance cost?

Excavation insurance costs vary based on business size, equipment value, project types, and coverage limits. Our customized policies typically save contractors 15-20% compared to standard construction policies while providing better coverage.

Does dirtwork insurance cover my heavy equipment?

Yes, our comprehensive dirtwork insurance includes specialized equipment coverage for excavators, bulldozers, backhoes, loaders, and other heavy machinery, protecting against theft, damage, and breakdowns.

Can I get coverage for environmental liability and contamination?

Absolutely. Our policies include options for environmental liability coverage, protecting your business from claims related to soil contamination, pollution incidents, and environmental cleanup requirements.

How quickly can I get a quote for my excavation business?

Most contractors receive a customized quote within 24 hours after completing our simple application. For urgent needs, we offer expedited same-day quotes for qualifying businesses.
