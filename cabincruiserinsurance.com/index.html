Frequently Asked Questions About Cabin Cruiser Insurance

What makes cabin cruiser insurance different from regular boat insurance?

Cabin cruiser insurance provides specialized coverage for live-aboard amenities, extended navigation, and higher-value equipment typically found on cabin cruisers. It includes coverage for personal belongings, overnight accommodations, and custom installations that standard boat policies may not adequately protect.

What areas are covered under the navigation coverage?

Our policies typically cover coastal waters, inland waterways, and major lakes across the United States. Extended navigation coverage can be customized to include international waters and specific cruising regions based on your needs.

How are personal belongings covered on my cabin cruiser?

Our policies include comprehensive coverage for personal effects typically kept aboard, including electronics, clothing, and recreational equipment. Coverage limits can be customized based on the value of your belongings, with options for scheduled items of higher value.

What emergency services are included?

Our policies include 24/7 emergency assistance, covering towing, fuel delivery, jump starts, and mechanical assistance. We also provide coverage for emergency lodging if your vessel becomes uninhabitable due to a covered loss.

How do I file a claim?

Claims can be filed 24/7 through our dedicated claims hotline or online portal. Our claims team specializes in cabin cruiser insurance and will guide you through the process, typically responding within 24 hours to begin processing your claim.
