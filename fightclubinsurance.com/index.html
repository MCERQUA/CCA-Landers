Frequently Asked Questions

What types of insurance do fighters and gyms need?

Combat sports professionals typically need:

  • General Liability Insurance
  • Professional Liability for instructors
  • Participant Accident coverage
  • Equipment and property insurance
  • Event liability coverage for competitions
  • Workers' Compensation for employees

Each provides different coverage for the unique risks in combat sports.

How much does combat sports insurance cost?

Costs vary based on:

  • Type and size of your facility
  • Number of participants
  • Training methods used
  • Location and state requirements
  • Coverage limits selected

Most gyms pay $1,500-$5,000 annually for comprehensive coverage, while individual fighters can get coverage starting at $300/year.

Does insurance cover sparring and competition injuries?

Our specialized policies explicitly cover:

  • Training injuries during sparring and drills
  • Competition injuries in sanctioned events
  • Medical expenses for participants
  • Liability protection for the gym

Standard insurance often excludes combat sports, which is why specialized coverage is essential.
